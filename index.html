Account Deletion – Finding You Again

We respect your right to control your data. If you'd like to delete your account, follow the steps below.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Finding You Again app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted

Your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile and account credentials
  • All journal entries and voice notes
  • Mood tracking history
  • Goals and progress data
  • Friend connections and leaderboard data
  • Notification preferences

What may be retained

  • Anonymised, aggregated usage data (not linked to your identity) may be retained for analytics purposes
Active subscription? If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through the Google Play Store before deleting your account to avoid future charges.
